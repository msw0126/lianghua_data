# US STOCKS SNAPSHOT-Wall St rises as top tech stocks hit record highs
NEW YORK, July 12 (Reuters) - U.S. stocks climbed on Thursday as top technology names hit record highs and industrials rebounded, offsetting worries about a U.S.-China trade war. 
The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 225.45 points, or 0.91 percent, to 24,925.9, the S&P; 500 gained 24.31 points, or 0.88 percent, to 2,798.33 and the Nasdaq Composite added 107.31 points, or 1.39 percent, to 7,823.92. (Reporting by Caroline Valetkevitch Editing by James Dalgleish)
